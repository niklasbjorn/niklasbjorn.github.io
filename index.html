About SBW 2016

The Swedish Bioinformatics Workshop is a two day conference held every year in Sweden at alternating university locations. The main target audience of SBW is PhD students in Sweden working in the diverse field of bioinformatics. At the event young researchers get to present their own projects in front of their peers, either as an oral presentation or in the form of a poster. Additionally, participants can choose from practical workshop sessions of their interest as well as to listen to high impact keynote speakers.

No registration fee for PhD students, postdocs and master's students.

Keynote lecturers at SBW 2016

Information about the confirmed keynote speakers will be announced here shortly.

Lennart Martens

Our heaven is their hell:
when data is easy, but results are not.

Dr. Lennart Martens is a professor of Systems Biology at Ghent University (Belgium). Dr. Martens foundation in bioinformatics lies in the analysis of the proteome but his current focus is on computational and data integration tools across multiple omics fields. Apart from numerous pertinent publications to the field Dr. Martens has co-authored textbooks on Proteomics Bioinformatics.
